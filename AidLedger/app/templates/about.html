{# Supervisor 1: Dr Shahbaz Siddiqui #}
SS

Dr. Shahbaz Akhtar Siddiqui

Associate Professor & Head of CYS/CE Department

Dr. Shahbaz Akhtar Siddiqui is a prominent academic leader currently serving as an Associate Professor and the Head of the Cyber Security & Computer Engineering Department at FAST-NUCES, Karachi. With a PhD in Computer Science, he bridges the gap between high-level research and practical application, specializing in Blockchain, Agentic AI, and Digital Forensics. Beyond the classroom, he is known for his collaboration with industry and government bodies, including work with the FIA on anti-money laundering initiatives, and he frequently leads specialized workshops to prepare students and professionals for the evolving tech landscape.

{# Supervisor 2: Mr Usama Antuley #}
UA

Mr. Usama Antuley

Lecturer & PhD Scholar

Mr. Usama Antuley is a dedicated Lecturer and PhD Scholar at FAST-NUCES Karachi, where he combines his teaching responsibilities with advanced research in Computer Science. With an academic background from NED University, his current scholarly work focuses on the intersection of Agentic AI, Blockchain, and Smart City infrastructure. He is recognized for his hands-on approach to research, particularly in developing machine learning solutions for local challenges like urban flood prediction, while actively mentoring students in the fields of cyber security and adaptive trust systems.
